Archiving Radio Today for Tomorrow

What Do I Do?

I archive radio broadcasting and programming from various stations across the United States (and potentially more in the future), because I believe it's in the best interest of society to have a record of the past, whether it be through written works, television, or radio. A lot of work goes into making this programming, and to have it simply be forgotten with time is a real shame.
